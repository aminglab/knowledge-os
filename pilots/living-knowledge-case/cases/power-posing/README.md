# Power Posing

This is the first public-facing case in the **Living Knowledge Case Pilot**.

## Why this case

The case is useful because it clearly shows the difference between:

- a static publication artifact,
- and a living knowledge object.

The original claim became widely visible through publication and popularization.
Later, the case accumulated replication failure, methodological criticism, internal withdrawal of support, and a weaker surviving descendant claim.

That makes it a good demonstration of:

- claim introduction,
- evidence attachment,
- dissent accumulation,
- verdict revision,
- claim splitting,
- and public snapshotting.
