## Current public reading

At this snapshot stage, the case reads as follows:

- the original strong-form claim no longer stands as a stable supported result,
- later empirical, internal, and methodological dissent significantly weaken it,
- the case splits into a weaker descendant claim about subjective feeling,
- and the descendant path remains contested rather than canonized.

## Why this snapshot exists

A static paper usually hides the difference between:

- the original claim,
- later dissent,
- and the present status.

A living knowledge snapshot should make that difference visible.
