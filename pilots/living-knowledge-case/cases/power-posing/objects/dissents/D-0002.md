# Dissent D-0002

## Summary

This dissent object represents the **2016** public withdrawal of support by **Dana Carney**, the first author of the original paper.

## Why this matters

This is not just another external criticism.
It marks a break in the original support structure itself.

In the current pilot interpretation, this is one of the strongest forms of dissent because it weakens the claim from within the original authorship chain.

## Working reading

The important point is not merely that one author changed her mind.
It is that the first author publicly indicated that she no longer believed the effect was real and pointed toward serious concerns about the credibility of the original result.

That makes D-0002 structurally different from a normal outside challenge.

## Attack surface

This dissent targets the credibility, continuing support status, and evidentiary confidence of the original strong-form claim.

## Consequence for the case

D-0002 helps explain why the pilot should not model the case as a simple dispute between one positive paper and one negative replication.
It shows internal collapse of confidence inside the original support structure.
