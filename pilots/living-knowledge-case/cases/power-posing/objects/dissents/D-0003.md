# Dissent D-0003

## Summary

This dissent object represents the later **2016** methodological attack associated with p-curve analysis of the power-posing literature.

## Why this matters

D-0003 is not the same kind of dissent as a failed replication.
It does not primarily say, "this one replication failed."
It says that the **evidentiary profile of the literature itself** does not look like what one would expect from a robust underlying effect.

That makes it a methodological and meta-evidential attack.

## Attack surface

This dissent targets the reliability of the apparent supporting literature around the original claim.
It therefore attacks the support structure at a different layer from D-0001.

## Consequence for the case

D-0003 matters because it helps prove that dissent in Knowledge OS cannot be modeled as a single undifferentiated bucket.

The case needs at least:

- empirical dissent,
- internal collapse of support,
- and methodological dissent.

This object is the methodological layer.
