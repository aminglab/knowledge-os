# Verdict V-0001

## Summary

This verdict records the current pilot interpretation that the original high-strength claim no longer stands in its initial form and that the case should be understood as split.

## Why this matters

The point of this verdict is not to settle all scholarly controversy forever.
Its point is to demonstrate that a claim can have a visible institutional state in a living system.

## Working judgment

- the original claim should not be treated as stably supported,
- the case should be modeled as weakened and contested,
- and the later history may continue through weaker descendant claims rather than through the original formulation.

## Notes

Later versions of this pilot may split this into multiple verdict objects, including:

- one verdict on the original claim,
- one verdict on a descendant claim about subjective feeling,
- and later snapshot-specific verdict states.
