# Verdict V-0002

## Summary

This verdict records the current pilot judgment on the weaker descendant claim represented by C-0002.

## Working judgment

The current pilot reading is:

- the descendant claim is **not fully stabilized**,
- but it remains more viable than the original strong-form claim,
- and the case therefore continues through a weaker surviving path rather than ending in total extinction.

## Why this matters

This verdict exists to prove that the system can represent **claim splitting**.

A real knowledge lineage often does not end in a clean binary outcome.
Instead:

- the original claim weakens,
- a narrower descendant survives,
- and each requires its own institutional judgment.

## Current stance

The descendant claim remains contested.
It is not canonized, not settled, and not treated as equivalent to the original claim.
But it has enough conceptual survival to deserve distinct representation.
