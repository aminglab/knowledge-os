# Claim C-0001

## Summary

This claim states that holding expansive, high-power body postures for a short period can change hormone levels and influence behavioral decision-making.

## Why this object exists

This is the original high-visibility claim at the center of the case.
It is the claim whose later lifecycle demonstrates the difference between static publication and living governance.

## Working assumptions

- short-duration embodied posture may affect psychological state,
- hormone changes can serve as part of the relevant mechanism,
- a laboratory intervention of this scale can produce measurable downstream effects.

## Notes

This object should remain distinct from weaker descendant claims about subjective feeling alone.
Those descendants may later be represented as separate claims in the same lineage.
